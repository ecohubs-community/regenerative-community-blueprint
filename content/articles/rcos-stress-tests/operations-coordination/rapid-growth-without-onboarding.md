**Failure Mode**

New members join without onboarding or probation.

**Layers Involved**

Layer 1 (Membership), Layer 5 (Operations)

**Relevant Invariants**

* 1.1 No implicit membership  
* 5.2 Operational knowledge is documented

**Test Condition**

* Members gain rights without a formal entry process.

**Expected RCOS Behavior**

* Mandatory onboarding and probation enforced.

**Pass Criteria**

* Membership status is explicit at all times.

**Fail Criteria**

* Culture and governance dilute silently.
